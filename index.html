Command Terminal

/fish
Cast your line into the water. Consumes stamina.
/blacksmith
Open the forge to unlock slots and equip Runes to your rod.
/worldboss attack
Deal damage to the currently active global World Boss.
/guild menu
Open your guild interface to manage members, quests, and buffs.
/shop
Buy baits, rods, and consumables to boost your journey.
/bag
View your inventory, fishes, chests, and runes.
